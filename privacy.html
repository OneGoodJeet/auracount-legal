At Big Yesh LLC, your privacy matters. AuraCount is a paid subscription app with no ads and no data selling. We collect only the data necessary to provide you with music listening analytics.

1. Introduction

Big Yesh LLC ("Company," "we," "us," "our") operates AuraCount, a music listening analytics application for iOS (the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use AuraCount.

By using AuraCount, you consent to the data practices described in this policy. This policy should be read in conjunction with our Terms of Service.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Sign in with Apple: Apple-provided user identifier, email address (may be a private relay address), and display name
  • Spotify Connection: Spotify user ID, display name, and profile image URL (authenticated via OAuth 2.0 with PKCE — we never receive or store your Spotify password)

2.2 Music Listening Data

The core of AuraCount's functionality requires access to your listening data:

Data Type Details
Track information Song name, artist name, album name, duration
Play metadata Timestamp, play count, listening duration in milliseconds
Artwork Album art URLs, artist image URLs (fetched from music APIs)
Imported history Spotify extended streaming history (JSON), Apple Music listening history (CSV/API)
Enriched metadata Platform played on, shuffle/offline mode, skip reason, playlist context (from Spotify extended history imports only)

2.3 Social & Profile Data

  • Profile information: Display name, profile visibility settings
  • Friend connections: Mutual friend relationships and associated listening comparisons
  • Contact hashes: If you opt in to friend discovery, phone number hashes (SHA-256) are used to match contacts — raw phone numbers are never transmitted or stored on our servers
  • Activity data: Leaderboard participation, friend activity visibility preferences

2.4 User-Generated Content

  • Ratings: Star ratings (0.5–5.0 scale) for tracks and albums
  • Shared content: Stat cards and recap images you choose to share externally

2.5 Device & Technical Data

  • Device type and operating system version
  • App version
  • Crash logs (for stability improvements only)

2.6 Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect: precise location data, financial/payment information (Apple handles all billing), browsing history, contacts (only hashed phone numbers for opt-in friend discovery), or advertising identifiers. We do not use any third-party analytics SDKs or advertising frameworks.

4. Data Storage & Infrastructure

4.1 Local Storage

AuraCount stores data locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. Local data includes your full listening history, cached analytics results, and app preferences. Local data is protected by your device's built-in encryption and access controls.

4.2 Cloud Storage

To enable social features and data backup, a subset of your data is synced to our cloud infrastructure powered by Supabase, which is hosted on Amazon Web Services (AWS). Cloud-stored data includes:

  • Listening history (for social comparisons and leaderboards)
  • Profile and account information
  • Friend connections and social preferences
  • Ratings
  • Milestones and recaps

Data syncs automatically on app launch, after new listening data is tracked, after data imports, and during background refresh cycles.

7. Data Security

We take the security of your data seriously and implement multiple layers of protection:

  • Authentication tokens: Encrypted at rest using pgcrypto with Supabase Vault and SECURITY DEFINER database functions
  • OAuth security: Spotify authentication uses PKCE (Proof Key for Code Exchange) — no client secrets are stored on your device
  • Local token storage: Sensitive tokens stored in the iOS Keychain, not in UserDefaults
  • Transport security: All network communication uses HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Database security: Row-level security (RLS) policies ensure users can only access their own data
  • Account deletion: Complete data removal via server-side edge function that purges all user data from all tables

While we strive to protect your information, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but are committed to implementing industry-standard practices.

10. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active and you maintain an active subscription. Specifically:

  • Active accounts: All data is retained while your account exists
  • Account deletion: When you delete your account, all cloud-stored data is permanently removed from our servers. Local data is cleared from the device.
  • Subscription cancellation: If you cancel your subscription but do not delete your account, your data remains stored but the app will not be accessible until you resubscribe
  • Crash logs: Retained for up to 90 days for debugging purposes, then automatically purged
